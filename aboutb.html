About Book Trekker

Welcome to Book Trekker

At Book Trekker, we believe in the power of words to inspire, educate, and transform lives. Our passion for books and literature is at the heart of everything we do. Since 1996 we've been serving our community by providing a curated selection of books that cater to every interest and age group.

Our Story

Book Trekker was founded by Priyanka with a dream of creating a haven for book lovers. What started as a small, independent bookstore has now grown into a beloved literary institution in Chennai. We owe our success to our unwavering commitment to promoting the joy of reading and the value of local, independent businesses.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a deep appreciation for books and reading in our community. We believe that books are more than just objects on a shelf; they are gateways to new worlds, sources of knowledge, and companions on life's journey. To achieve this mission, we: Curate a diverse selection of books across genres and interests. Create a welcoming and inclusive space for book enthusiasts. Host author events, book clubs, and other literary activities. Support local authors and publishers. Provide exceptional customer service to help you find the perfect book.

The Book Trekker Experience

When you step into Book Trekker, you'll be greeted by the comforting scent of fresh pages and the sight of countless books waiting to be explored. Our cozy reading nooks and inviting atmosphere make it the perfect place to lose yourself in a book. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to offer recommendations or engage in literary conversations.

Community Involvement

We take pride in being an active member of our community. We regularly partner with local schools, libraries, and charitable organizations to promote literacy and a love for reading. Our bookstore serves as a hub for book-related events, author signings, and book launches, fostering a sense of connection among book lovers.
